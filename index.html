Built by one builder, used in 100+ countries

Xnipertools started in mid-2025 with a simple frustration: most "free" tool websites bury a 10-second job under sign-up walls, watermarks and upload queues. So we built our own — one dashboard of fast, genuinely free tools that respect your time and your privacy.

Every tool here is designed, coded and tested in-house — nothing is a rebranded plugin. And because almost everything runs directly in your browser, your photos, PDFs and passwords never leave your device: there is simply no server for them to be uploaded to. That's not a marketing line, it's the architecture.

The collection grows almost every week, usually from ideas readers send through the request page — the passport photo maker, the school toolkit and the debt payoff calculator all started as user requests.

Common questions

Are these tools really free?

Yes — every tool on Xnipertools is completely free, with no trial periods, usage limits or locked features. You will never hit a paywall halfway through a job.

Are my files uploaded to a server?

No. Image, PDF and text tools run entirely in your browser using client-side technology. Your files stay on your device — when you close the tab, nothing of yours is left behind.

Do I need to create an account?

Never. There is no sign-up, no login and no email required for any tool. Tools that save your work (like the Weekly Planner) store it locally on your own device.

Do the tools work on mobile?

Yes — every tool is built mobile-first and tested on phones before launch. You can also install Xnipertools as an app from your browser menu for one-tap access.

Who builds Xnipertools?

Xnipertools is an independent project by the builder behind XniperBuilds. Every tool is designed, coded and tested in-house — you can read the full story on the About page.

How accurate are the calculators?

Financial and health calculators use the standard published formulas, which are shown on each page. They are reliable for planning and education, but for big decisions we always recommend confirming with a qualified professional.

Can't find the tool you need?

Request a new tool, or a change to an existing one. We review every idea, and approved tools go live every week.
