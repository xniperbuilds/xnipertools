Why use our Image Upscaler?

Small or low-resolution image? This free Image Upscaler & Enhancer enlarges it up to 4× using high-quality step-by-step upscaling, then applies smart sharpening to bring back crisp edges and detail — so your enlarged image looks clean instead of blurry. Great for making product photos, logos, thumbnails and old pictures bigger and sharper.

It all happens right in your browser using HTML canvas, so it's fast, completely free, needs no sign-up, has no watermark, and your image is never uploaded. Just pick how much to enlarge and how much to sharpen, then download.

Is the image upscaler free?

Yes — completely free, no sign-up and no watermark.

Are my images uploaded?

No. Everything is processed locally in your browser; your image never leaves your device.

How much can I enlarge an image?

Up to 4× the original, capped to keep results sharp and fast, with high-quality upscaling plus sharpening.
