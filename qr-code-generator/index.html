Why use our QR Code Generator?

Our free QR Code Generator turns any link, text, Wi-Fi password or contact detail into a scannable QR code in seconds — right inside your browser. Unlike many online tools, nothing is uploaded to a server: your data is processed locally on your device, so it stays completely private.

What makes it different is control over how your code looks. Choose from multiple dot shapes — square, rounded, dots, classy and extra-rounded — pick any foreground and background colour, apply a futuristic gradient theme, or drop your own logo in the centre. You can export a high-resolution PNG (up to 1024×1024) that's crisp enough for posters, packaging, business cards and billboards.

QR codes are everywhere: menus, payments, product packaging, event check-ins, Instagram bios and WhatsApp links. A clean, well-branded code helps people trust and scan it. Because our generator uses high error-correction, your codes stay reliable even with a logo in the middle or a little print damage. No sign-up, no watermark, no limits — generate as many codes as you like, free forever.

Can I use these QR codes for business?

Yes. Every code you create is free for personal and commercial use — menus, flyers, packaging, marketing, anywhere.

Do these QR codes expire?

No. These are static QR codes, so they never expire and keep working forever, as long as the link they point to stays live.

How to download the QR code?

Generate your code, then tap Download PNG to save a high-resolution image to your device. You can also export an SVG for print.
