Request a Tool

Got an idea for a new tool — or a change to one we already have? Tell us, and we'll review it.

Xnipertools is shaped by the people who use it. If there's a tool you wish existed, or something you'd improve in an existing one, send it our way. Every request is read and seriously considered — the most useful ideas become real tools on the site.

How your request becomes a tool

Here's the exact journey every request goes through:

1

Submit

You send your idea using the form below — it only takes a minute.

2

Review

We review every request for usefulness, demand and feasibility. This usually takes a few days.

3

Approval

If your idea is a good fit, it's approved and added to our build queue. We may email you to confirm details.

4

Build & Test

The tool is carefully built and tested to meet our quality and speed standards.

5

Launch

Once it passes testing, it goes live — new and updated tools are released every Sunday.
