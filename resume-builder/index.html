About this Resume Builder

This free resume builder helps you create a clean, recruiter-friendly CV in minutes — no design skills needed. Fill in your contact details, summary, experience, education and skills, watch the live preview update, choose an accent colour, then download a print-ready PDF with no watermark.

How to use it

  1. Fill in your contact details and a short summary.
  2. Add your experience, education and skills.
  3. Pick an accent colour and click Download PDF.

Is this resume builder free?

Yes — completely free, no signup, and the downloaded PDF has no watermark.

Can I download my resume as a PDF?

Yes — click Download PDF for a clean, print-ready file you can email or upload to job sites.

Is my resume data private?

Yes. Your resume is built entirely in your browser and never uploaded anywhere.
