Why use our Password Generator?

Weak and reused passwords are the number one reason accounts get hacked. This free Password Generator creates long, random passwords that are extremely hard to guess or brute-force — and it does it right inside your browser using a cryptographically secure random generator (crypto.getRandomValues), so your password is never sent to or stored on any server.

You're in full control: choose a length from 4 to 48 characters, and mix uppercase, lowercase, numbers and symbols. Turn on "exclude similar characters" if you want to avoid easy-to-confuse letters like the lowercase L and the number 1. The live strength meter shows how tough your password is, and one tap copies it to your clipboard.

How to use it

  1. Set the length with the slider (16+ is recommended).
  2. Choose which character types to include.
  3. Tap Generate (or ↻ for a new one).
  4. Tap Copy and paste it where you need it.

Are these passwords safe to use?

Yes. They're generated locally with a secure random generator and never uploaded or saved anywhere.

Are the generated passwords stored anywhere?

No. Nothing is saved. Refresh or close the page and the password is gone — so copy it before you leave.

What makes a strong password?

Length and variety. Use at least 12–16 characters mixing all four types, and never reuse a password across different sites.
