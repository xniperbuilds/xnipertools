Why use our Meme Generator?

Make a meme in seconds — no design skills needed. Upload any image (a photo, a screenshot, or a frame from your gameplay), then add as many text boxes as you like. Drag each one anywhere on the image and give it its own font, size, colour and outline. Add your own watermark — text or a logo — to brand it, then download a high-quality PNG ready for WhatsApp, Instagram, TikTok or your group chat.

Everything is rendered right in your browser with HTML canvas, so it's fast, completely free, needs no sign-up, and your image is never uploaded anywhere. Perfect for gaming memes, reaction pics and trend templates you can share in a tap.

How to use it

  1. Upload an image (or drag & drop it).
  2. Tap ➕ Add text, type, and drag it where you want.
  3. Pick a font, colour and outline for each text.
  4. Optionally add a watermark (text or your logo).
  5. Tap Download Meme — done!

Is the meme generator free?

Yes — completely free, no sign-up, no limits. Make as many memes as you want.

Can I move text and use different fonts?

Yes. Add multiple text boxes, drag each anywhere, and give each its own font, size, colour and outline.

Can I add my own watermark?

Yes — add a text watermark or upload your logo, set size and opacity, and drag it into place.

Are my images uploaded to a server?

No. Your meme is created locally in your browser; the image never leaves your device.
