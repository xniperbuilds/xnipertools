About the FF Tournament Standings Calculator

Running a daily Free Fire tournament with 10–15 teams? This free tool builds your standings in seconds. Enter each team's name, position points and kills — the calculator adds the totals and automatically ranks every team from #1 down, awarding gold, silver and bronze to the top three. Add your organization's name and logo, then download a clean, professional standings image to share with players and on your channels.

How ranking works

Teams are sorted by total points (position points + kills). If two teams have the same total, the team with more position points ranks higher. The top three teams get 🥇 🥈 🥉.

Optional stats

You can also turn on extra columns — total damage, matches played and survival time. They only appear in your standings and image if you switch them on, keeping the result clean.
