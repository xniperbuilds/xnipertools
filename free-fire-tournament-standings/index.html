About the FF Tournament Standings Calculator

Running a daily Free Fire tournament with 10–15 teams? This free tool builds your standings in seconds. Enter each team's name, position points and kills — the calculator adds the totals and automatically ranks every team from #1 down, awarding gold, silver and bronze to the top three. Add your organization's name and logo, then download a clean, professional standings image to share with players and on your channels.

FAQ

How are the standings ranked?

Teams are ranked by total points, where total = position points + kills. If two teams have the same total, the team with more position points ranks higher. The top three teams automatically get gold, silver and bronze medals.

Can I add my own tournament name and logo?

Yes. Enter your organization or tournament name and upload an optional logo. Both appear on the downloadable standings image so the result looks professional and branded to your event.

What extra columns can I show?

You can optionally turn on Booyah wins, total damage, matches played and survival time. These columns only appear in the standings and image when you enable them, keeping the result clean if you do not use them.

Is my tournament data uploaded anywhere?

No. Everything runs in your browser and your teams, scores and logo are saved only on your device using local storage. Nothing is uploaded to any server, so your data stays private.
