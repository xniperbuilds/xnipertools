About this Percentage Calculator

A fast, free percentage calculator for the three things people need most: finding a percentage of a number, finding what percent one number is of another, and working out a percentage increase or decrease. Type your numbers and the answer appears instantly — no button, no signup, and it works perfectly on your phone.

How to use it

  1. Pick a mode: % of a number, X is what %, or % change.
  2. Enter your two numbers.
  3. Read the answer — with the working shown — straight away.

How do I calculate the percentage of a number?

To find X% of Y, multiply Y by X and divide by 100. For example, 20% of 150 = 150 × 20 ÷ 100 = 30.

How do I find what percent one number is of another?

Divide the first number by the second and multiply by 100. So 30 is 30 ÷ 150 × 100 = 20% of 150.

How do I calculate percentage increase or decrease?

Subtract the original from the new value, divide by the original, then multiply by 100. From 100 to 150 is a 50% increase; from 150 to 120 is a 20% decrease.
