About this tool

The WhatsApp Direct Link Generator turns any phone number into a click-to-chat wa.me link — with an optional message ready to send. Share it in your bio, website, ad, or status and people can message you on WhatsApp instantly, without saving your number first. Perfect for businesses, online sellers and support.

How to use it

  1. Enter the phone number with its country code (for example +92 for Pakistan, +91 for India).
  2. Optionally type a message you want pre-filled in the chat.
  3. Tap Generate Link.
  4. Copy your wa.me link, or tap Test on WhatsApp to try it.
  5. Paste it anywhere — bio, website, poster or status.

Is it safe?

Yes. Everything runs locally in your browser — your number and message are never uploaded, stored or shared. The generated link uses WhatsApp's official wa.me format.

Does it work on mobile?

Absolutely. The link opens WhatsApp on Android and iPhone, and WhatsApp Web/Desktop on computers — for anyone who has WhatsApp installed.
