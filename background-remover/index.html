Why use our Background Remover?

Cut out the background of any photo in seconds — no design skills, no Photoshop. This free AI Background Remover automatically detects the main subject (a person, product or object) and erases everything behind it, leaving a clean transparent PNG you can drop onto any new background. Great for online store product shots, profile pictures, stickers, thumbnails and logos.

What makes it special: the AI runs entirely in your browser. Your photo is never uploaded to a server, so it's completely private — and there's no sign-up and no watermark. The first time, your browser downloads the AI model once (about 40 MB); after that, every image is fast.

Is the background remover free?

Yes — completely free, no sign-up and no watermark.

Are my photos uploaded to a server?

No. The AI runs on your own device, so your photo never leaves your browser.

Why does it download something the first time?

It downloads the AI model once (~40 MB). After that it's cached, so the next photos are much quicker.
