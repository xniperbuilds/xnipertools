Why use our Spin the Wheel?

The Spin the Wheel tool is the fun, fair way to pick a random name from a list. Add your entries, hit spin, and watch the wheel land on a winner. It's perfect for giveaways and raffles, choosing who goes first in a game, classroom random picks, team selections, or just settling "who's paying?" — and it's completely free with no sign-up.

Because the winner is chosen with your browser's cryptographically secure random generator, every name has an exactly equal chance — so your draws are genuinely fair. Hosting a giveaway? Turn on "Remove winner after spin" to keep spinning for multiple unique winners, and screen-record the spin so your audience can see the result is real and transparent.

How to use it

  1. Type or paste your names in the box — one per line.
  2. Tap SPIN (the centre button or the big one below).
  3. The wheel lands on a random winner 🎉.
  4. For multiple winners, switch on Remove winner after spin and spin again.

Is the wheel truly random and fair?

Yes. It uses secure randomness, so nothing is rigged and every entry has an equal chance.

Can I use it for giveaways?

Definitely — paste your participants, spin, and record it. It's a clean, transparent way to draw winners.

Can I add my own names?

Yes, add as many as you like. Your list is saved in your browser for next time.
