About Xnipertools

Free, fast and beautiful tools for everyone.

Xnipertools is a growing collection of free online tools built to make everyday tasks quick and effortless. From calculators to generators to gaming tools, our goal is simple: give you the tool you need, instantly — with a clean, modern experience that feels great on any device.

What we offer

  • Calculators — age, percentages, loans and more, with clear results.
  • Generators — stylish text, passwords, QR codes and other handy creators.
  • Gaming tools — stylish name generators and helpers for Free Fire, PUBG and more.

Our mission

We believe simple tools should be fast, free and beautiful. No clutter, no forced signups, no nonsense — just open a tool and get your result. Every tool is designed mobile-first, because that's where most of you are.

Who builds this?

Xnipertools is built and maintained by Xniper, an independent maker focused on crafting simple, genuinely useful tools. New tools are added regularly based on what people actually need.

Got an idea?

We're always adding new tools. If there's something you'd love to see, tell us here — your suggestion might be the next tool we build.
