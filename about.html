About Xnipertools

Free, fast and beautiful tools for everyone.

Xnipertools is a growing collection of free online tools built to make everyday tasks quick and effortless. From calculators to generators to image and gaming tools, our goal is simple: give you the exact tool you need, instantly — with a clean, modern experience that feels great on any device. There's nothing to install, no account to create and no charges. You open a tool, get your result and get on with your day.

What started as a handful of small utilities has grown into a full toolbox of dozens of tools across calculators, converters, image editing, productivity, generators and gaming. Everything lives in one fast, lightweight website so you don't have to hunt through a dozen different sites — or download a separate app — every time you need to do something simple.

What we offer

Our tools are organised into a few easy categories so you can always find what you need:

  • Calculators — work out your age, BMI, percentages, loan and EMI payments and more, with clear, instant results.
  • Converters — switch between units and currencies quickly, using up-to-date conversions.
  • Image & AI tools — compress images, remove backgrounds, upscale photos and make passport-size pictures right in your browser.
  • Productivity tools — build invoices and résumés, plan your week, and create test papers and ID cards with our school tools.
  • Generators — create QR codes, strong passwords, memes, WhatsApp links and stylish text in seconds.
  • Text & developer tools — count words, convert case, format JSON, pick colours and more.
  • Gaming tools — stylish name generators and helpers for Free Fire and other popular games.

Our mission

We believe simple tools should be fast, free and beautiful. So many online tools are buried under pop-ups, forced signups, paywalls and confusing layouts. We're building the opposite of that — open a tool and get your result, no friction in the way. Every page is designed mobile-first, because that's where most of our visitors are, and everything is tuned to load fast even on a slow connection.

How our tools work

Wherever possible, our tools run directly in your browser. That means the data you type or the images you upload are processed on your own device and are not stored on our servers. It keeps things fast, works even when your connection is patchy, and respects your privacy. You can read exactly how we handle data in our Privacy Policy.

Why we built Xnipertools

The idea was simple: there should be one place you can trust for the small jobs that pop up every day — resizing an image, generating a QR code, checking a percentage, building a quick invoice. Instead of ten different sites cluttered with ads and sign-up walls, Xnipertools puts genuinely useful tools in one clean, fast home that anyone can use for free.

Who builds this?

Xnipertools is built and maintained by Nazim, the independent maker behind the XniperBuilds studio — the same builder responsible for the XniperArcade browser games and several desktop and mobile apps. Every tool on this site is designed, coded and tested in-house; nothing here is a rebranded plugin or a white-label kit. It's an independent project, not a corporate platform — which means feedback lands in the builder's own inbox, and new tools are added regularly based on what people actually ask for. You can follow the work on Instagram.

Our editorial standards

Two rules apply to everything we publish. First, tools must be tested before they ship — on desktop and on real phones, in light and dark mode, before they appear on the homepage. Second, content must earn its place: our guides use real formulas, worked examples and screenshots of our own tools, and when we compare approaches (like debt payoff strategies) we run the actual simulation and publish the exact numbers. Calculators state their formulas and their limits, including clear disclaimers on financial and health topics, so you always know what a number means and what it doesn't.

What's next

We add new tools almost every week and keep improving the ones that are already here. Our roadmap is shaped directly by visitor requests, so the more you tell us, the better Xnipertools gets.

Got an idea?

We're always adding new tools. If there's something you'd love to see, tell us here — your suggestion might be the next tool we build. You can also request a tool or report a bug any time.
