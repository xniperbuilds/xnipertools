// questions

FAQ

Is XCipher really anonymous?

Yes. There's no sign-up, email or phone number. Every time you join you get a fresh random alias, and no personal data is stored.

Can anyone read my messages?

No. Messages are end-to-end encrypted in your browser before they ever leave your device. The server only relays unreadable ciphertext — even we can't read them.

Do messages get saved?

No. Messages and rooms are ephemeral — they vanish when the timer ends or the room closes. Nothing is kept permanently.

Is it free?

Yes. Public, temporary and private rooms are all available for free.
