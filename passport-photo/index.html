Make passport size photos online — free

The Passport Size Photo Maker turns any photo into print-ready passport, visa or ID photos in under a minute. Pick an official size, drag your face into the frame, fine-tune brightness and skin tone, optionally remove the background — and download a 4×6 inch sheet with 8 photos (on A4 the same 4×6 block sits top-left with cutting guides, rest of the page stays blank). One 4×6 print at any photo shop costs a fraction of a studio visit.

How to use

  1. Upload a clear, front-facing photo (good light, neutral expression).
  2. Pick a size — 35×45mm passport, 2×2 inch US visa, ID card, 1×1 inch or custom.
  3. Drag & zoom your photo inside the locked frame, then apply the crop.
  4. Adjust brightness, contrast, warmth — or remove the background to plain white.
  5. Choose the 4×6 sheet, download as JPG/PDF and print at 100% scale.

Official photo rules (quick checklist)

  • Plain white / off-white background, no shadows.
  • Neutral expression, both eyes open, mouth closed.
  • Face centered, covering roughly 70–80% of the frame height.
  • No glasses glare, no head covering (unless religious), recent photo (under 6 months).

FAQ

What size is a standard passport photo?

Most countries require 35×45mm (about 1.38×1.77 inches). The USA requires 2×2 inches for passport and visa applications.

How many passport photos fit on a 4×6 sheet?

For 35×45mm photos, 8 copies fit on one 4×6 inch sheet (4 columns × 2 rows) with standard spacing.

Can I use this for official passport or visa applications?

Yes — if you follow the official size requirements and use a plain white or off-white background. Always verify the exact rules with your issuing authority.

Is my photo stored or uploaded anywhere?

No. All processing happens locally in your browser — your photo never leaves your device.
