A free all-in-one toolkit for teachers

Stop fighting with Word tables and broken formatting. This free test paper generator lets a teacher create a clean, professional question paper with proper sections, marks and instructions, then print it or save it as a PDF. Switch between several paper templates, write in English, Urdu or Arabic, and toggle a colourful design or an ink-saving black-and-white version for the photocopier.

Beyond exam papers you also get an MCQ worksheet maker with an optional answer key, an OMR bubble answer sheet generator for any number of questions, and an exam date sheet maker. Each tool is designed to be quick for non-technical teachers: fill the fields, watch the live preview, and download. More modules — lesson planners, applications, result cards, attendance sheets and admit cards — are on the way.

Frequently asked questions

Can I make papers in Urdu?
Yes. Choose Urdu and the whole paper flows right-to-left in Nastaliq script, with field labels like Name, Roll No, Total Marks and Time translated automatically. Arabic is supported too.

How do I get a PDF?
Click Print to save directly as a PDF from your browser, or Download PDF for a ready file. Pick a colourful template or the clean black-and-white version that saves ink when you print.

Is it really free?
Yes, 100% free with no signup. Every tool runs in your browser and nothing you type is uploaded anywhere.
