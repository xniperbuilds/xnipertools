Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

At Xnipertools ("we", "us", "our"), accessible from xnipertools.com, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected and how it is used when you visit our website.

Information we collect

Our tools work right in your browser. You do not need to create an account, and we do not ask for personal details to use any tool. Any data you type into a tool (for example, a date or a name) is processed on your device and is not stored on our servers.

If you choose to subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address (through our email provider, MailerLite) for the sole purpose of sending you updates about new tools. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email we send.

Cookies

Like most websites, Xnipertools may use "cookies" — small files stored on your device — to improve your experience and to support advertising. You can disable cookies anytime through your browser settings.

Advertising & Google AdSense

We may display ads served by third-party vendors, including Google AdSense. These partners may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

  • Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies (including the DART cookie) to serve ads to our users.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites.
  • You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Analytics

We may use analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use our site — for example, which tools are most popular. This information is aggregated and does not personally identify you.

Third-party links

Our site may contain links to other websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external sites.

Children's privacy

Our tools are intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will remove it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, email us at xniperbuilds@gmail.com.
