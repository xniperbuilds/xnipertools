About the Free Fire Name Generator

Want a cool, stylish name for Free Fire that stands out in the lobby? This generator turns your normal name into dozens of fancy versions using special fonts, symbols and decorations. Just type your name, and tap any style to copy it — then paste it straight into your Free Fire profile.

How to change your name in Free Fire

  1. Type your name above and tap copy on the style you like.
  2. Open Free Fire → tap your profile → Edit.
  3. Paste the copied name and save (you may need a Name Change Card).

Are these stylish names safe to use?

Yes — these are just normal Unicode characters and symbols, so they work in Free Fire, PUBG, BGMI, Instagram, TikTok and most games and apps.
