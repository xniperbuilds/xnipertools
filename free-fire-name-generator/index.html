About the Free Fire Name Generator

Want a cool, pro-style name for Free Fire? This generator turns your normal name into dozens of stylish versions using special fonts, symbols and decorations — the same styles you see on the top of the ranked leaderboard. Type your name, optionally add a clan tag, then tap any style to copy it and paste it into your Free Fire profile.

How to use it

  1. Type your name, and add a clan tag if you want (it joins with a メ, like pro players).
  2. Browse the styles, or use Pick a font to choose one exact font.
  3. Tap Copy, then paste it into Free Fire → Profile → Edit name.
  4. Tap ★ to save your favourites for later.

How do I make an invisible Free Fire name?

Free Fire removes normal spaces, so an invisible or blank name needs a special character. Tap Copy Blank Space above and paste it as your name for a fully invisible name — or turn on Invisible space to keep real spaces between words in your stylish name.

Are these stylish names safe to use?

Yes — these are normal Unicode characters and symbols, so they work in Free Fire, PUBG, BGMI, Instagram, TikTok and most games and apps.
