About the Free Fire Points Calculator

This tool adds up your Free Fire tournament points across all your matches. For every match, pick your placement (rank) and the number of kills — the calculator instantly shows that match's points and your running grand total. When you're done, add your team name and tap Download Result (JPG) to get a clean image you can share with your squad or post online.

How points are counted

Placement points: #1 (Booyah) = 12, #2 = 9, #3 = 8, #4 = 7, #5 = 6, #6 = 5, #7 = 4, #8 = 3, #9 = 2, #10 = 1, #11–12 = 0. Each kill = 1 point. Total = placement points + kill points across every match.

Does it save my data?

Your matches are saved right in your browser, so your numbers stay even if you close the page. Nothing is uploaded.
