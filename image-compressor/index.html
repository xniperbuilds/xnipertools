About this Image Tool

One free tool for everything you need to do with an image: compress it to a smaller size, resize its dimensions, convert between JPG, PNG and WEBP, crop or rotate it, and add a text or logo watermark. Everything happens right inside your browser — your image is never uploaded to any server.

How to use it

  1. Upload an image (or drag & drop it).
  2. Rotate, flip or crop it if you want.
  3. Pick an output format, drag the quality slider, or set a new width/height.
  4. Tap Download — you'll see exactly how much smaller it got.

Is it private?

Yes — your image is processed locally on your device. It is never sent or stored anywhere.
