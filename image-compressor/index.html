About this Image Compressor

Need to shrink an image for WhatsApp, email or a website? This free tool compresses, resizes and converts your images instantly — and everything happens right inside your browser, so your photos are never uploaded to any server.

How to use it

  1. Upload an image (or drag & drop it).
  2. Pick an output format — JPG for the smallest size, WEBP for great quality at low size, or PNG for lossless/transparent images.
  3. Drag the quality slider, or set a new width/height to resize.
  4. Tap Download — you'll see exactly how much smaller it got.

Is it safe and private?

Yes — your image is processed locally on your device using your browser. It is never sent or stored anywhere.
