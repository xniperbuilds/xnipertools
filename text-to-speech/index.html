Text to Speech

Paste any text, pick a voice, set the speed and pitch, and listen — it reads aloud right in your browser.

💡 Want more voices? Add them in your device settings — Android: Settings → Text-to-Speech; iPhone: Settings → Accessibility → Spoken Content → Voices. New voices show up here automatically. ⭐ = higher-quality / natural voices.

🔒 100% Private: your text is spoken on your device and never uploaded.

Why use our Text to Speech?

This free Text to Speech (TTS) tool reads any text out loud in a natural voice — perfect for proofreading your writing by ear, learning pronunciation, listening to articles or notes hands-free, accessibility, or turning a script into a voiceover you can screen-record. Just paste your text, choose a voice, and press play.

You're in control of the delivery: pick from the voices installed on your device, then fine-tune the speed, pitch and volume. As it speaks, the current word is highlighted so you can follow along. Everything runs locally using your browser's built-in speech engine, so it's fast, free, works with no sign-up, and your text never leaves your device.

How to use it

  1. Type or paste your text.
  2. Choose a voice and adjust speed, pitch and volume.
  3. Press Play — use Pause, Resume or Stop any time.

Is the text to speech free?

Yes — completely free, no sign-up, no limits. It uses your browser's built-in speech engine.

Can I download the audio as MP3?

It reads the text aloud live in your browser and doesn't export a file. If you need to save it, screen-record the playback.

Which voices are available?

The voices come from your own device, so the list varies by phone or computer. Most devices include several English voices and other languages.
